Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and creamy ricotta cheese, baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. In a pan, cook ground beef with garlic and onion, then add tomato sauce.
  4. In a baking dish, layer the noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses.
  5. Repeat layers and top with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
